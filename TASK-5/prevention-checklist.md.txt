## 📱 Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students

* Always use **strong and unique passwords** for different accounts, and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
* Think before clicking on links or downloading attachments, especially from unknown emails or messages.
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts like banking or email.
* Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
* Do not share personal information like OTPs, passwords, or ID details with anyone online.
* Be mindful of what you post on social media—oversharing can expose you to risks.
* Verify job offers, internships, or online opportunities before applying to avoid scams.
* Log out from shared or public computers after using your accounts.

### 💳 Financial / UPI Safety Tips

* Never enter your **UPI PIN to receive money**—it is only required for sending money.
* Do not scan unknown QR codes or accept payment requests from strangers.
* Always double-check the receiver’s details before making any payment.

---

### 🚨 In Case of Cybercrime

* Report immediately on the **National Cyber Crime Portal: [https://cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)**
* Call the **Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930** for quick assistance

---

**Note:** Staying alert and informed is the best way to protect yourself in the digital world.
